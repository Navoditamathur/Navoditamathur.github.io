Primarily developed classification machine learning model which could predict the most suitable crop to grow. A web app was developed to input and store details like address, the products (seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, insecticides, tools) and their prices. The user/farmer is required to enter the details like region, temperature, rainfall, altitude, if the land was irrigated etc., and the webapp predicts the crop best suited to grow, given the conditions. It also provides a list of users providing the equipment required. Used MySQL database to store the registered users, Python for decision tree classification and android application to help guide further."

Introduction:
-------
In the pursuit of a simpler and more fulfilling life, an increasing number of individuals are turning to farming and gardening as not just activities but as cherished hobbies. Embracing the soil, cultivating plants, and tending to crops have become more than just pastimes; they symbolize a desire to reconnect with nature, adopt sustainable practices, and find solace in the simplicity of nurturing life.

In this contemporary age where urbanization often distances people from the natural world, the trend of adopting farming and gardening as hobbies reflects a profound longing for a harmonious coexistence with the environment. Beyond the conventional understanding of agriculture as a profession, there is a growing community of enthusiasts who view cultivating the land as a therapeutic and rewarding endeavor.

The allure of growing one's food, witnessing the miracle of seeds transforming into flourishing plants, and reaping the tangible rewards of one's efforts contribute to the appeal of farming and gardening as hobbies. This cultural shift is not merely about producing sustenance; it encapsulates a desire for hands-on engagement with the earth, an appreciation for the seasons, and a commitment to sustainable living.

The project aims to make farming and gardening more accessible to the common man.

Project Objective:
-------
The project aims to revolutionize farming and gardening by bringing these practices directly to the doorsteps of the common man. This initiative considers various factors such as climate conditions, soil quality, irrigation, flooding risks, geographical area, altitude, and market demand.

Proposed System:
-------
* User Registration: Individuals interested in cultivating crops or plants register on the website. They provide details such as temperature, rainfall, and soil pH.
* Tailored Recommendations: The system processes user inputs and generates personalized crop recommendations, considering the specified conditions. Users receive detailed information about the suggested crops via SMS.
* Companion App: An accompanying mobile app offers specific care instructions for the recommended crops. This feature ensures that users have access to comprehensive guidance throughout the cultivation process.
* Dealer Registration: Dealers specializing in agricultural materials like seeds, fertilizers, and tools can register on the platform. They can offer their products to users as needed.

Deliverables:
-------
A comprehensive and user-friendly website will be delivered, allowing users to:
* Register and express interest in growing specific crops or plants.
* Access personalized recommendations based on their geographical and climatic conditions.
* Receive SMS notifications containing detailed information about the recommended crops.
* Download a companion app providing precise care instructions for the selected crops.
* Enable dealers to register and sell necessary agricultural materials.
